Hi, I'm Barbara.
I'm a certified English teacher and front-end developer in training. Originally, I'm from Krakow, Poland, however, I've been living abroad for the last 5 years.

In my free time I enjoy cooking and baking - it helps me relax and clear my smind. When I need a creativity bust I make different kinds of handcrafts sewing, embroidery, jewellery.
My biggest passion is travelling! Mostly solo and mostly on a budget and I've made it my lifestyle. For the last few years I have been living out of my backpack!

Take your time and explore my website to learn more about me!

English Classes

I got my CELTA certificate (Certificate in English Language Teaching to Adults). A few months later I got my first student and I have been teaching English online ever since, mostly to private students.

I take pride in my individual approach. I might not be a native speaker, but I'm definitely an experienced language learner myself and I know how hard learning can be. I try to build rapport with my students and keep them motivated.

If you'd like to learn English or improve your current skills, check my profile on Preply or contact me directly.
