English Teacher, Amateur Baker, Full-Time Traveller

I'm a certified English teacher and front-end developer in training. Originally, I'm from Krakow, Poland, but I've been living abroad for the last 5 years.

In my free time I enjoy cooking and baking - it helps me relax and clear my mind. When I need a creativity bust I make different kinds of handcrafts , however, my biggest passion is travelling! Mostly solo and mostly on a budget. For the last few years I have been living out of my backpack!
