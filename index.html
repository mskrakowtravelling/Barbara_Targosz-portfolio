As a final project of my SheCodes Plus workshop I designed and coded a fully functional weather app using JavaScript and Axios. It was styled in CSS with elements of Bootstrap. The source of weather data is OpenWeather.

You can search for a city or you can use your current location to display both the current weather and 5 days forcast. The current date and time of the given location as well as your current date and time are displayed on the website. It is hosted on a free platform, therefore, it doesn't have a unique web address.
